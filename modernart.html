Welcome to the World of Modern Art

I am intersted in Modern Art, so I created my own project above the topic which was named "BACK to MODERN ART". In this website, you will find three of my favorite modern artists.

Yves Klein

Yves Klein was a French artist and an important figure in post-war European art. He was a leading member of the French artistic movement of Nouveau réalisme founded in 1960 by art critic Pierre Restany. Klein was a pioneer in the development of performance art, and is seen as an inspiration to and as a forerunner of minimal art, as well as pop art.

Abramović

Marina Abramović is a Serbian conceptual and performance artist, philanthropist,writer, and filmmaker.Her work explores body art, endurance art and feminist art, the relationship between the performer and audience, the limits of the body, and the possibilities of the mind. Abramović refers to herself as the "grandmother of performance art".

Cai Guo-qiang

Cai Guo-qiang is a Chinese artist who currently lives and works in New York City and New Jersey. Cai's work is mainly inspired by traditional Chinese culture. His practice draws on a variety of symbols, narratives, traditions and materials. These include fengshui, Chinese medicine, shanshui paintings, science, flora and fauna, portraiture, and fireworks.
